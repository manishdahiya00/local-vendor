Executive Summary

We're building the next-generation hyperlocal delivery platform that transforms how communities access goods and services. By connecting customers directly with local riders through an intelligent subscription model, we're creating a sustainable ecosystem that benefits everyone.

🎯

Mission Statement

Democratizing local commerce through instant delivery while empowering riders with flexible earning opportunities and sustainable platform economics.

02

Business Model

Our three-sided marketplace creates value for customers, riders, and local businesses through seamless integration and smart economics.

🚀 Ecosystem Players

  • 👤
    Customers: Access to instant local delivery with transparent pricing and real-time tracking
  • 🛵
    Riders: Flexible earning opportunities with subscription-based access and wallet management
  • 🏪
    Local Shops: Extended reach and digital presence without complex integrations

💰 Revenue Architecture

  • 🔄
    Rider Subscriptions: Tiered monthly plans optimizing for different usage patterns
  • 📊
    Delivery Premiums: Dynamic commission structure based on distance and demand
  • ✨
    Convenience Layer: Service fees that enhance customer experience
  • 📈
    Business Solutions: Premium features for shops including promotions and analytics
